HOW TO BECOME HAPPY? 5 WAYS TO BE HAPPY


What is meaning of 'being happy'? 'Being Happy' means when you feel good, joyful, satisfied or pleasure and your mood is good, you like to do your work with satisfaction. Everyone has own perspectives or ideas of being happy and defining happiness. Happiness depends on our thoughts. It does matter what we think and how we think about anything.
James Allen says on book 'As a man thinketh',

A person is literally what he/she thinks, his character being the complete sum of all his thoughts.


Advantages of being happy


  • It promotes healthy lifestyle
  • Productivity increases
  • Doing work with happiness makes you satisfied
  • It leads to stay young and long. Yes, you have heard it right. Was written in book 'IKIGAI', people never really retire, who keep doing what they love to do for as long as their health allows.
  • It helps to combat stress, reduce pain and boost immune system.

Disadvantages of 'Not Being Happy'

  • Health issues
  • Productivity decreases
  • Chances increase to adopt an addiction
  • It leads to tension and nervous breakdown

What Makes Happy


  • Yoga or meditation
  • Positivity
  • Self care
  • Passion for life
  • Less social media
  • A good environment
  • Optimism
  • Exploring nature
  • Writing
  • Reading a good book
  • Spirituality

What Makes Unhappy


  • Comparison with others
  • Body shaming
  • Negative thoughts
  • Living with bad company
  • Pessimism
  • Not taking care of yourself
  • Mental health avoidance
  • Too much social media
  • Self doubt
  • Being materialistic

Ways to Find Happiness


There are so many ideas and ways to explore in regards to find happiness. Below you'll find some tips to help you find your way of being happy.


Find A New Hobby


Hobby, it gives endless happiness. It is the best way to develop a positive attitude and achieve happiness.


Taking Care of Yourself


Taking care of yourself daily can impact your mood, stress level, concentration and productivity. It allows yourself a moment to recharge and to be your best self.


Exercise


When we do exercise, the chemical releases chemicals that boost our mood and suppress harmones that causes anxiety and depression.


Eat a Healthy Diet


Improving your diet may help to improve your mood, give you more energy, help you think more clearly.


Be Grateful


Be grateful is a great attitude to be happy all the time. you become grateful, when you notice that something good has come into your life and it makes you happy.


CONCLUSION


'Being Happy' is an attiutude. If you become happy, it becomes a habit and it makes your personality better. So, be happy all the time.
