WE'RE UNIQUE

We are players,
but not of same game.
We climb a tree,
but we are not monkey.
We try to swim,
but we are not fish.
We are different players,
but we play the same game.
We are unique,
but we want to achieve same things.
This is because of our surroundings.
People say do that,
Because everyone is doing so.
But why they don't think,
Fish doesn't need to climb a tree,
And bird doesn't have to dive into the water.

We are special,
That's why we must choose our special goals.

GANESH KUMAR
